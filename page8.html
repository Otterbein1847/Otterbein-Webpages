🎓Here are some Phishing Tip:

  • 📬 If a wild scholarship appears in your inbox like a Pokémon — and you never applied for it — don’t click. That’s how cyber-scammers catch you instead.

  • 🎉 Real scholarships don’t arrive with countdown timers, flashing confetti, or “CLAIM NOW” buttons. If it looks like it was designed by a used car salesman in 2003, it’s probably bait.

  • 🔗 Always double-check links, verify the sender, and trust your gut — especially when someone offers free money out of nowhere.

  • 🕵️‍♀️ If you suspect an email might be a phishing attempt, please click the Phish Alert button in your Outlook (see image below) to report it.
