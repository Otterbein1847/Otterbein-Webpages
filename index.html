🛡️ Outsmart the Phish: Pro Tips to Stay Safe

  • 🕵️‍♀️ Trust, but verify: A familiar name doesn't mean it's a real sender. Always double-check.
  • 🔍 Hover before you click: Sneaky links can be masked. Preview the URL first.
  • ⏰ Feeling rushed? If the email feels urgent, that’s your cue to slow down — phishers thrive on panic.
  • 📎 Attachment anxiety: Unexpected .zip or .exe files? Treat them like ticking time bombs.
  • 💬 Spell check the sender: Weird grammar or misspellings? Probably not from your IT department.
  • ❌ Don't Just Click! Just because it says "Otterbein" doesn’t mean it’s trustworthy — check before you act.
  • 📩 See something fishy? Use the Phish Alert button in Outlook to report suspicious emails.
  • 🧑‍💻 Need backup? If you're unsure or need a second opinion, reach out to the experts.
