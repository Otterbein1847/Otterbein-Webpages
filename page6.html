🧠 Real Tips (So This Doesn’t Happen Again):

  • Think before you click: If it smells fishy 🐠, it probably is.
  • Double-check the sender: No legit company is emailing from @outlook123.biz
  • Hover over links: Is it sending you to a sketchy site? Abort mission.
  • Urgency is bait: “ACT NOW OR ELSE” = red flag 🚩
  • Trust no attachment: Especially if it ends in .exe, .zip, or .docm
